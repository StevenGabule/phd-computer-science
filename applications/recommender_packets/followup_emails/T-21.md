# Recommender Follow-up Email — T-21 (3 weeks before deadline)

**When to send:** Three weeks before each application's deadline. For Dec 1 deadlines, send Nov 10. For Dec 15 deadlines, send Nov 24.

**Purpose:** Polite reminder + supply the program-by-program list with portal links. This is the recommender's "no surprises" notice.

---

**To:** [Recommender's email]
**Subject:** Re: PhD letters — program list and deadlines for upcoming round

---

Dear Professor [Last Name],

Thanks again for agreeing to write letters of recommendation for me. The first deadlines are three weeks away ([DATE]), so I wanted to share the program-by-program details.

I am applying to [N] programs. Here are the ones with deadlines in the next 4 weeks; I have arranged each in date order:

| # | Program | Deadline (recipient TZ) | Submission method | Portal status |
|---|---------|-------------------------|-------------------|---------------|
| 1 | [Program A] | [Date, time, TZ] | [Portal direct / Interfolio / email] | [Account created; invitation will arrive by [date]] |
| 2 | [Program B] | [Date, time, TZ] | [Portal direct] | [Already invited; please check your inbox / spam for an email from `*.applyweb.com`] |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

For programs using direct-portal invitations: each portal sends you a unique upload link when I name you as a recommender. Most arrive within an hour; if any is missing, please tell me and I will re-send the invitation.

For programs accepting Interfolio: my Interfolio dossier is at [link]. The letter can be uploaded once and dispatched to all participating programs.

Two notes:

1. **Format**: most portals accept a 1-2 page PDF. A few request letterhead; I have flagged those in the table above. No portal in this list has a hard word limit.

2. **Confidentiality**: by default, all letters are confidential (FERPA waiver auto-applied). I have not requested copies.

If anything is unclear, or if a portal is misbehaving on your side, please tell me — I would much rather know about a problem at T-21 than at T-2.

I will send one more reminder at T-7 with any portals that haven't received your letter yet. After that I won't bother you unless something is wrong.

Thank you again — I know writing 20+ near-identical letters is a real cost, and I appreciate it.

Best,
John Paul L. Gabule
johnpaullimgabule@gmail.com

## Customization checklist

- [ ] Fill in the program table with actual rows (subset relevant to this 4-week window)
- [ ] Include portal status per program (most useful info)

- [ ] Send to all recommenders simultaneously (each gets their own personalized table)
- [ ] Add a calendar reminder for T-7 follow-up

## Tone notes

- Calm, organized, in-control. Recommenders who get panicky T-21 emails ("OMG only 3 weeks!!") start to dread the writer.
- Don't apologize for the workload — but acknowledge it once.
- Don't include the SOP or CV again; they have the packet from the original ask.
- Brevity is respect.
