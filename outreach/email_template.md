# Cold Email Template (v1, May 2026)

Replace bracketed placeholders before sending. Customize the specific-paper reference per recipient.

## Subject lines (test both)
- "Question on your [paper title] from a prospective PhD applicant"
- "[Your problem statement keyword] — feedback request from a prospective applicant"

## Body

> Dear Prof. [Last name],
>
> I'm a Master's-level researcher preparing to apply for PhD CS programs in the Fall 2027 cycle (for Fall 2028 entry). Your recent paper, "[exact title]," particularly the [specific observation, 1 sentence — prove you read it], directly informs work I'm planning on agentic retrieval-augmented generation for legal-document analysis.
>
> I'm developing a 1-page problem statement on [your specific subtask in 1 sentence] and would value your honest 60-second reaction — particularly whether the framing is sound and whether you see obvious issues with [specific decision in your problem statement, e.g., dataset choice]. The draft is attached.
>
> I understand your time is limited; even a one-line response would be valuable.
>
> Thank you for considering it.
>
> Sincerely,
> John Paul L. Gabule
> [Current role / affiliation]
> johnpaullimgabule@gmail.com
> [Link to public research blog or GitHub once set up]

## Customization checklist before each send
- [ ] [Last name] replaced with recipient's actual last name
- [ ] [exact title] is the exact title of one of their recent (2024-2026) papers
- [ ] [specific observation] proves you actually read the paper (not just the abstract)
- [ ] [your specific subtask] is the locked subtask from `project/problem_statement.md`
- [ ] [specific decision] names a concrete choice in your problem statement
- [ ] Attached PDF is the current locked version of `project/problem_statement.md`
- [ ] Logged in `outreach/log.md` immediately after sending

## Sending guidance
- Target send time: Tuesday or Wednesday morning in recipient's timezone
- Avoid: Friday afternoon, Monday morning, holidays
- Expect 10-20% response rate; do not interpret silence as rejection
- Wait 2 weeks before a single polite follow-up
